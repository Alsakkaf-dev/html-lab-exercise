Technical Methodology

For the transition to Lab 2, I migrated all image alignment from the legacy align attribute to our new .floated-img CSS class. This separation of concerns creates a cleaner DOM and centralizes our control over visual placement.

By explicitly setting width and height attributes on every media asset, I ensure that our Grid and Sidebar layouts remain stable while images load, fulfilling the best practices for user experience and Core Web Vitals.
